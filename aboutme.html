My name is Sanne Michelle Duvier. I am 31 years old, and I live in Odense. Currently I am studying multimedia design.

I am adventurous, exploratory, challenging and stubborn. I enjoy travelling and partaking in larger outdoor events. Basically I prefer making memories over materialistic possesions; which is why I have developed a passion for photography and videography.

For a while I have been filming and editing travel vlogs. It is mainly a hobby, but it is what motivated me to become a multimedia designer. I enjoy creating and/or editing visual content for people to enjoy.

“Find a job you enjoy doing, and you will never have to work a day in your life.” ― Mark Twain

Designing visual content, mainly for travel and outdoor events promotion, both for online and offline communication is what I would love to do for a living.
